About Goose Farm Investing

Helping investors grow their asset portfolio like a goose farm

Our Mission

To help all goose farm owners measure the value of their asset geese and execute good investments.

Our Story

Welcome to all asset goose farm owners who are raising various asset geese.

We support and encourage the journey of beginner goose farmers growing into free and wealthy goose farm owners.

We believe that the method of good investment to become a free and wealthy goose farm owner is to discover assets you believe in, measure their value, buy them at a low price, and hold them while generating cash flow.

Our goal is to share insights and information on valuing various asset geese, including real estate, US stocks, domestic stocks, gold, cryptocurrencies, and startups, so that asset goose farm owners can understand good investment methods and implement them consistently.

About

A free investor and asset goose farm owner.

After graduating from Yonsei University with a degree in Business Administration, I have been consistently growing my asset goose farm by applying good investment methods to various assets such as real estate, stocks, gold, cryptocurrencies, and startups, based on my experience as a stock analyst for about 13 years at GE, Nomura Securities, Shinhan Investment Securities, Korea Investment Securities, and Citigroup Global Markets Securities, and numerous practical investment experiences.

After publishing The Rediscovery of Investment in 2021 and Free Investor in 2022, we started operating the Goose Farm Investment Platform to support the investment execution of goose farm owners who sympathized with our goose farm investment principles.
